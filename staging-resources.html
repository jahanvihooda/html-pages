Financial Support

The FES Awards Office provides information and advice on applying for student funding. Whether you are interested in scholarships, research grants or financial aid bursaries, be sure to take advantage of our faculty-wide, university-wide and external resources. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact the Student Resource Assistant. Please visit Faculty of Graduate Studies – Funding and Awards for more information, including internal and external scholarship opportunities.

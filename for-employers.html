For Employers

FES graduates have the diverse skills necessary to meet the challenges of your evolving workplace. Our distinctive interdisciplinary programs provide a range of perspectives from the social sciences, humanities and sciences, focus on connections between natural, built, social and organizational environments and combine critical study with practical experience.

Get assistance recruiting our students

Whether you need to recruit full-time or part-time employees, summer students, or interns, FES Career Services can help you achieve your goals. Please see below for the various ways we can provide assistance – contact Student Resource Assistant for additional information or to discuss arrangements.

Recruitment for immediate job openings

We’re happy to advertise your employment, field experience or volunteer positions free of charge on our online Career Opportunities Board. For instructions on how to submit a posting, please see below.

Application screening

As part of the job posting process, you can choose for us to collect résumés and send them to you. If you wish, we can also rank the candidates before sending along their information.

On-campus interview facilities

We can make room bookings and other arrangements for you to interview candidates on campus.

Information sessions

The more students know about your organization, the more likely they are to respond to your recruitment efforts. Many organizations come to FES to make presentations as well as to specifically recruit students. We take care of room arrangements and promoting your session.

How to post jobs at FES

FES Career Services has an online Career Opportunities Board where openings can be promoted to students. If you are interested in posting an opportunity, there are many options:

  • Use our online job posting form
  • Fax the posting to us at 416-736-5679 using the Job Order Form
  • Email your posting to fesworks@yorku.ca

Once we receive the job description, we will prepare a posting for you. If you have specific advertising needs or wish to discuss an existing program, please phone the Student Resource Assistant at 416-736-2100 ext. 33196.

How students can reply to your posting

As part of the posting form you will be asked whether you wish interested students to contact you directly or for us to collect the résumés and fax or mail them to you.
